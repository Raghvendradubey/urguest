Important Points For Property Owners

Caretaker will manage to get tenants. The caretaker will get the verification done for all tenants. The caretaker will have to keep all the documents related to tenant/tenants with him and the owner can check the same at any suitable time. The owner can provide reasonable logic for not taking a tenant or terminating a tenant. Caretaker can sign on behalf of the owner on rent-agreement with tenant. The caretaker will collect rent from all the tenants. A separate financial understanding will be had with Caretaker

The caretaker will deposit all bills of electricity, power backup , RWA maintenance etc except the house tax, which will be paid by the owner.

Caretaker does not become owner of the property in any way and similarly the caretaker does not have any of the owners liabilities

That the owner can visit the said premise at a reasonable time after discussion.
